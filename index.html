FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do Web3Forms work?

Web3Forms provides an API endpoint for your HTML Forms, so that you can start receiving form submissions to your website forms. There is no backend code involved. You only need to put two lines and it’s compatible with your current tools and technology.

Why is the Pro plan so cheap?

Web3Forms is developed with the latest technologies to cut costs so that more people can use our services. Only a fair price to cover our costs plus a little more for our time. We still don’t know why other competitors are charging so high.

Is the free plan really free?

Yes, you can use the free plan as long as you needed without even creating an account. Only use your email address to get form submissions. However, we might suspend your account if we detect any abuse or misuse of this service.

I lost my Access Key. What should I do?

If you lost the access key, don't worry. Just enter your email again to generate Access Key and we will send it to you again.

What happens if I exceed the submission limit?

We don't put any hard limits on any accounts. However, we might send an email to notify you about the same and ask you to upgrade.

How do I cancel my Pro plan?

You can go to the “Subscription Manager” link in the first email you’ve received. Or, just contact us and we will do that for you.

Do you store data submitted through my form?

Form data that is send to the endpoint is forwarded to your email address. We don’t store any data from your forms on our server. However, we might store the logs in Amazon Web Services for debugging purposes.

Is my email address secure with you?

Yes, we will not share your information with any third party. We use your email to send data submitted from the form to your email. However, we may contact you to share updates and information about our service.
